02 Inside the Studio

Life at RedOrbit.

We are an unusual mix of veterans and first-shippers, working out of Colombo with collaborators across three continents. Here is what shapes the day-to-day.

Time to do it right

We protect deep-work blocks aggressively. Meetings are concentrated on Tuesdays and Thursdays so makers can hold a creative line through the rest of the week.

A real studio, not a Slack

Our Colombo office is the gravitational centre — quiet edit bays, a dedicated playtest room, and the kind of espresso machine that justifies its existence.

Genuinely global work

We co-develop with publishers in Tokyo, London, and Los Angeles. Senior roles include travel; junior roles include the network that will define your decade.

Compensation, plainly

Salaries are benchmarked against regional senior bands and reviewed honestly each year. Profit share on shipped titles. Equipment budget is generous and renewed on cycle.

Credit where it's earned

Everyone who contributes to a shipped title is named in its credits — full stop. We support attendance at GDC and regional showcases for shipping team members.

A culture of critique

Weekly review sessions where work is discussed openly and structurally. The bar is high; the conversation about how to clear it is collegial.
